# Customer Project Lifecycle

## Phase 1: Discovery and Plan
- Define business goal, audience, and success metric.
- Capture constraints and compliance requirements.
- Produce a dated master plan.

## Phase 2: Build
- Implement smallest end-to-end valuable slice.
- Validate interfaces and failure behavior.
- Update plan and risk log continuously.

## Phase 3: Hardening
- Add validation, rate limiting, and observability.
- Verify dependency and supply-chain safety.
- Complete compliance content and consent flows.

## Phase 4: Launch
- Execute release runbook.
- Complete smoke tests and evidence capture.
- Assign on-call/owner and rollback path.

## Phase 5: Operate and Improve
- Review telemetry and customer feedback.
- Patch issues by severity.
- Feed lessons back into templates.
